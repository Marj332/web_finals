Lechon belly

Lechon belly is a popular Filipino dish that consists of a whole pig's belly, deboned and stuffed with various herbs, spices, and aromatics, then roasted until crispy and golden brown. It is a beloved dish in the Philippines and is often served during special occasions and celebrations, such as fiestas, family gatherings, and holidays.
